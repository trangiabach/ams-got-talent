General information

Central Highlands, a land with the breath of greatness. Through two wars against the French and the Americans, the people of the Central Highlands wrote many epic versions of solidarity and heroic spirit against foreign invaders to gain freedom for the Fatherland. Speaking of the Central Highlands, we cannot help but mention the sincere people of Bom Bo who are faithful to growing wheat and staying up at night and pounding rice to feed the army. We also cannot forget the hero Nup, whose revolutionary life became an immortal epic. You are invited to listen to the two works "Tieng chay tren soc Bom Bo" composed by Xuan Hong and "Hat mung anh hung Nup" by musician Tran Quy to feel the love expressed through the heroic history pages of the indomitable Central Highlands.
